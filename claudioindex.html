Fun Facts about Marina Bay Sands (MBS)

MBS began operations on 27 April 2010 with a phased opening, and officially opened on 23 June in the same year MBS has been one of LVS’s most profitable properties.

About Marina Bay Sands (MBS)

Marina Bay Sands (MBS) is an integrated resort (IR) with a hotel, casino, retail mall, as well as convention facilities and entertainment venues including theatres, nightclubs and a museum. Sitting on a 570,000 sq m waterfront site at Marina Bay, MBS is operated by Las Vegas Sands (LVS) Corp.
