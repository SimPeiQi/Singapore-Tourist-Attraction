Ancient Egypt

Universal Studios Singapore's Ancient Egypt zone is a popular attraction that transports visitors back in time to the mysterious and ancient civilization of Egypt. The zone features several rides, such as the Revenge of the Mummy roller coaster, which takes visitors on a thrilling journey through dark tombs and pyramids. Additionally, there are several shows and performances that take place in the zone, such as the Lights, Camera, Action! Special Effects Show, which allows visitors to see the behind-the-scenes magic of filmmaking. Overall, the Ancient Egypt zone at Universal Studios Singapore offers a unique and exciting experience for visitors, allowing them to learn about and explore the ancient civilization in an interactive and thrilling way.
