Far Far Away

Universal Studios Singapore's Far Far Away zone is a fairytale-inspired area that features attractions, live shows, and character meet-and-greets based on the Shrek movies. Visitors can explore the colorful streets of Far Far Away and enjoy dining and shopping options. It's a fun and immersive experience for all ages.
