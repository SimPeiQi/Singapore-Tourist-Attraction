Universal Studios Singapore's New York section is a stunning recreation of the Big Apple's iconic architecture and landmarks. The section also features exciting rides and attractions such as the Sesame Street Spaghetti Space Chase, a fun-filled family ride with beloved Sesame Street characters. In addition, the New York section is home to a variety of dining options, including authentic New York City-style pizza and hot dogs, as well as street performances and meet-and-greets with popular characters. Whether you're a fan of the city or just looking for a unique experience, Universal Studios Singapore's New York section is a must-visit.
