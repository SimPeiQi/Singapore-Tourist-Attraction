Welcome To
The Lost World

Universal Studios Singapore's The Lost World zone is a prehistoric-themed area that features thrilling rides such as the Jurassic Park Rapids Adventure, Dino-Soarin' and Canopy Flyer. The area is beautifully designed with realistic dinosaurs, lush greenery and waterfalls that make visitors feel like they've been transported to a different time and place. Visitors can also find a variety of dining and shopping options like the Jurassic Outfitters store. It's a must-see for any fan of the Jurassic Park franchise.
