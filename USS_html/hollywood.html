Welcome To Hollywood

Universal Studios Singapore's Hollywood zone features iconic sets and rides inspired by Hollywood's biggest blockbusters, such as Battlestar Galactica, Revenge of the Mummy, and Sesame Street Spaghetti Space Chase. Visitors can also enjoy street performances, meet and greets with beloved characters, and delicious Hollywood-inspired dining options.
