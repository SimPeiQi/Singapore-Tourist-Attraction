Welcome To
Sci-Fi City

Universal Studios Singapore's Sci-Fi City is an out-of-this-world experience, featuring some of the most thrilling and technologically advanced rides in the park. Visitors can take a journey through time and space on the iconic Battlestar Galactica: HUMAN vs. CYLON roller coaster, or experience the intense action of the TRANSFORMERS The Ride: The Ultimate 3D Battle. With its cutting-edge technology and futuristic design, Sci-Fi City transports you into the world your favorite science fiction movies and shows. The zone also offers a variety of dining and shopping options, as well as meet and greets with beloved characters from the TRANSFORMERS franchise.
